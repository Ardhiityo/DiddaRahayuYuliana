About Myself

I am Didda Rahayu Yuliana, M.Kom — a dedicated lecturer and academic leader with over 15 years of experience in higher education. Currently, I serve as the Head of the Informatics Engineering Study Program at Universitas Al-Khairiyah, where I focus on curriculum development, student mentorship, and academic quality assurance. My academic background is rooted in Informatics Engineering, with teaching expertise in subjects such as Object-Oriented Programming, Human-Computer Interaction, Multimedia, and Digital Image Processing. I have contributed to numerous research projects and publications, particularly in the areas of education technology, CSR-based community development, and academic information systems. In addition to teaching and research, I actively participate in professional training and certification programs, including PEKERTI and LSP, and I am involved in various academic organizations such as APTIKOM, IAII, and ASKII. My mission is to foster inclusive, innovative, and impactful learning environments that prepare students to thrive in the digital era.

Academic Contributions

As a lecturer and academic leader, I am committed to advancing informatics education, mentoring students, and contributing to research and community development through technology and innovation.

Student Mentorship

Supervised over 30 undergraduate theses and guided students toward academic and career success through personalized mentoring.

Curriculum Development

Led curriculum design initiatives aligned with OBE, SKKNI, and KKNI standards to enhance program relevance and graduate competencies.

Academic Leadership

Served as Head and Secretary of Informatics Study Programs, managing accreditation, quality assurance, and academic planning since 2015.

Research & Publications

Published multiple journal articles and conference papers on informatics, education, and community development, including CSR and digital systems.

Professional Training

Completed certified training in curriculum design, quality assurance, and competency assessment, including PEKERTI and LSP certifications.

Community Engagement

Led and participated in community service programs across Banten, focusing on digital literacy, entrepreneurship, and vocational empowerment.
