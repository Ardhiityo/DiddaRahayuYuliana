Vocational Life Skills Program

This program was designed to equip final-year students with practical life skills and entrepreneurial knowledge to support their transition into the workforce. Conducted in Pandeglang in 2020, the initiative included workshops on business planning, communication, financial literacy, and digital tools for self-employment.

  • Facilitator: Didda Rahayu Yuliana, M.Kom
  • Institution: Universitas Al-Khairiyah
  • Type: Vocational Community Service
  • Completed: 2020

The program included workshops on business planning, communication strategies, financial literacy, and the use of digital tools to support self-employment. Participants—primarily final-year students—were guided through practical exercises to develop entrepreneurial mindsets and essential life skills for entering the workforce.

This initiative reflected a commitment to preparing students for real-world challenges beyond the classroom. It supported Universitas Al-Khairiyah’s mission to foster independent, skilled graduates who are ready to contribute to their communities through innovation and resilience.
